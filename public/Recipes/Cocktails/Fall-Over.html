Fall Over

This autumnal libation was created by my father, who currently resides in Florida. I think he may have been missing the crisp fall air of the great northeast. Or perhaps he was just feeling particularly festive. Either way, it's quite delicious, and a great answer to the oft-asked question: "What do I do with maple flavored whiskey?" (A query I used to hear a lot of in my days of working in a liquor store.)

Ingredients

  • 2 oz maple flavored whiskey, such as Crown Royal Maple, or Cabin Fever
  • 2-4 oz fresh apple cider
  • Club soda
  • Cinnamon sugar

Preparation

  1. Prepare a rocks or highball glass by wetting the rim with apple cider, and then dipping in a mixture of cinnamon and sugar.
  2. Fill glass with crushed ice.
  3. Pour whiskey and cider into glass and stir.
  4. Top with a splash of club soda.
  5. Garnish with an apple wedge.
